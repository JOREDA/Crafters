# The Crafters - Handcrafted Excellence

![The Crafters Logo](././src/assets/logo.png)

The Crafters is a premier e-commerce platform showcasing exquisite handcrafted products created by skilled Indian artisans. Our mission is to bring traditional craftsmanship to the modern world while supporting local artisans and promoting sustainable practices.

## About Us

The Crafters is dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich handicraft heritage. We curate a unique collection of handmade products that combine traditional techniques with contemporary designs. Our platform serves as a bridge between skilled artisans and discerning customers who appreciate authentic handcrafted items.
